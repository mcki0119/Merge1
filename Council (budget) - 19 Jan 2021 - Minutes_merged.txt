2021 Budget-Operating Budget-City Departments

Moved by: Councillor Kalivas

Fullarton, Councillor Journal, Councillor Kalivas, Councillor

For:

The vote on the original motion, as amended, was now taken and the
‘motion, as amended, was carried.

Amended Motion
Moved by: Councillor Fullarton

THAT Council receive report SR2021-5 2021 User Fee Analysis for
Information; and,

‘THAT Council approve the 2021 proposed user fees as attached with the
following modifications;

THAT Brockville Cemetery fees for interment rights and all related
services (except for regulated fees) be increased by 3%; and

THAT Island camping and island mooring rates be removed from the
2021 schedule of user fees pending review and report to Council in
April 2021; and

THAT staff continue to work on the analysis of user fees and report back to
the Finance and Administration Committee in 2021; and,

THAT the necessary by-laws be enacted.

Mayor Baker, Councillor Bursey, Councillor Earle, Councillor
Fullarton, Councillor Journal, Councillor Kalivas, Councillor
Lavergne, Councillor Wales, and Councillor Wren
