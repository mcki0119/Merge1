2022-42
Requested Exemption to the City of Brockville Noise By-law
VIA Rail - 141 Perth Street

THAT VIA Rail Canada Inc. be granted an exemption from the City of
Brockville Noise By-law to permit construction activities to occur at the VIA
Rail Station located at 141 Perth Street, between the hours of 7:00 pm and
5:00 am from 15 May 2022 to 8 August 2022. The work to be performed
is in relation to the replacement of the VIA Rail Platform, improvements to
the parking area and service connections to Perth Street, which will require
temporary lane closures; and,

THAT Staff prepare the Certificate of Approval with respect to the exemption
for execution by the Mayor and Clerk.

Joint Services Committee, Housing and Affordability Task Force
United Counties of Leeds and Grenville

THAT the correspondence from the United Counties of Leeds and Grenville
regarding the Housing Affordability Task Force recommendations be
received; and

THAT the City of Brockville approve, in principle, the recommendations
outline in the United Counties of Leeds & Grenville Report HA-013-2021 -

2022-37
Harbour Dock Replacement Project - Update II

THAT staff report SR2022-37 Harbour Dock Replacement Project - Update
II be received; and

THAT Council direct staff to prepare a draft plan and policy regarding
complimentary transient day-docking for Council consideration; and

update.
