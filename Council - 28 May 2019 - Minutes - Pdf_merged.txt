MAYOR'S REMARKS

Mayor Baker noted regrets from Councillor Fullarton and Councillor Earle.

On May 23rd the City hosted a delegation from the Republic of Korea in a cultural
exchange which included a wreath-laying in remembrance of the Korean War
organized by the Brockville Legion. It was part of the South Korean Embassy's
recent efforts to reach out to communities around Ottawa to enhance cultural,
economic and interpersonal ties.

Mayor Baker invited all citizens to take part in the Pride events scheduled for the

weekend of June 7th. It was also announced that on June 6th, 10:00 am at City
Hall the Brockville Rifles will be unveiling a display commemorating D-Day.

Councillor Lavergne and Councillor Bursey announced the recipients of the 2019
Legacy funds.
