MAYOR'S REMARKS.

Mayor Baker noted that he attended a meeting with eight Mayors of municipalities
in Leeds and Grenville to discuss flooding and water control. The group will
continue to meet to be prepared for the anticipated spring floods and high-water
levels.

His Worship sent congratulations to the Volunteer Bureau on Dancing Stars of
Leeds and Grenville fundraising event held this past weekend. It was also mention
that the Thousand Islands Film Festival which was hosted by Film Brockville, made
a come back this weekend after a 14-year hiatus.

Special recognition was given to Neal Condron, Brockville native. Neal won several
medals while completing in the cross-country skiing events at the Special Olympics
Canada 2020 held in Thunder Bay at the end of February.

DISCLOSURE OF INTEREST

No. 2020-058, 2020 Budget - Final Levy, clauses 1, 2 and 4; and Staff Report No.
2020-055, Tourism Information and Stakeholder Services Contract as his

employer may bid on and/or provide services in relation to projects funded by
these approvals.
