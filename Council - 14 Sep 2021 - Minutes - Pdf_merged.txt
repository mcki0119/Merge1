MAYOR'S REMARKS.

Mayor Baker noted that the Brockville Tunnel will be closing for the season on
‘Thanksgiving Monday, October 11th. He also noted that the Halloween event was
cancelled this year. He reminded the community that there is a vaccination centre
available at the Health Unit as well as local pharmacies. Vaccination rates for our
area continue to be high. The City Hall Clock Tower refurbishment is complete. The
unveiling will occur later this month,

His Worship announced that public input is being sought on the design of Reynolds
Park as well as the Official Plan Update. He encouraged residents to contact
councillor or staff with suggestions and questions or watch the City’s social media
accounts for further details.
