LAND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT STATEMENT

MAYOR'S REMARKS.

Mayor Wren welcomed everyone to the meeting and wished all a happy St.
Patrick's day week. He passed along congratulations to the board and staff of the
‘Aquatarium for a successful gala event on Friday evening. The washrooms on
blockhouse island will close on March 20 for updates and renovations, the facilities
will reopen around the end of the month. With the warmer weather approaching,
staff are working hard to fill potholes as they arise and residents are encouraged
to clear snow away from their drains when possible.

His Worship announced that there will be a celebration to open the Railway Tunnel
for the 2023 season. The event will be held at 2:00 pm Thursday, April 6, and
everyone is welcome to attend.
