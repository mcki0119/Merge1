Economic Development and Planning Committee (EDP) Minutes -
Tuesday, April 04, 2017

Councillor LeSueur asked for clarification regarding the ability for someone
to advertise for another business, for example at a plaza. A. McGinnis
responded that this is possible but there is only ane sign per property.
Councillor LeSueur wondered if there was any stipulation in the by-law
regarding brightness of the electronic signs. A. McGinnis stated that there is
not they could add it but it would be very hard to control.

Councillor Earle disagreed with 5.10 (d) which prohibit off-site advertising on
the Electronic Message Boards (EDM).

Councillor Earle would like to see 5.10 (f) changed so that they are
permitted to use EMBs as part of a portable sign.
