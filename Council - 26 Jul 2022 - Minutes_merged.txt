MAYOR'S REMARKS.

Mayor Kalivas remarked that it is good to see the amount of activity that is
happening downtown, patios and stores are busy, and there are many events
still to come this summer.

He encouraged all residents to be good ambassadors for the city as there are
‘many tourists in town. His Worship noted Councillor Earle is absent this evening
as he is out of town.
