2023-50
Establishment of a Municipal Heritage Committee

THAT City Council hereby dissolve the Heritage Brockville Local
Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee and;

THAT By-law 283-89 being a by-law to establish a Local Architectural
Conservation Advisory Committee be repealed and;

THAT Section 28 of the Ontario Heritage Act, 1990, as amended, provides
that the Council of a municipality may, by by-law, establish a municipal
heritage committee to advise and assist the Council on all matters relating
to Parts IV and V of the Ontario Heritage Act and;

THAT City Council establish a Brockville Municipal Heritage Committee and
adopt the Terms of Reference for said Committee.
CARRIED
