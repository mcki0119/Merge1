MAYOR'S REMARKS.

Mayor Kalivas welcomed everyone to the meeting. He noted Councillor Lavergne
will not be in attendance at the meeting tonight. Mayor Kalivas bestowed
condolences to the family of Dennis Gleason and the family of Claudette Williams.

His Worship noted that a special meeting will be held on Wednesday, November
24th to hear from applicants who are interested in the vacant council positions.
The River of Lights Festival commences on Friday, November 26th. The Santa
Claus stationary parade will be held on Saturday, November 27th. He encouraged
the community to shop local this year in support of the retailers who have suffered
during the pandemic.
