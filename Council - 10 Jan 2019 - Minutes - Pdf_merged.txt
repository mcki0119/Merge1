2019 Budget Update
Incremental Items
D. Dick presented the list of incremental items.

R. Nolan spoke to the incremental request for the BAC Box Office
Coordinator position.

C. Cosgrove explained that the $20,000 for the Aquatarium is to add to the
building maintenance program for the maintenance of the condominium
unit at Tall Ships Landing. He also explained that the arena budgets
Increased by approximately 13% due to the provincial wage legislation but
the ice rental fees have only been increased by 2%. He is seeking direction
with regard to the ice rental fee percentage increase.

G. Pigeon presented information regarding the replacement of the fire
truck and a combined police/fire boat. Councillor Fullarton requested that
the number of “on water” call history be provided.

S. Fraser provided an overview of the Police Board items,
