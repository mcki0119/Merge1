‘STAFF REPORTS
1. 2014-18-11
“Brockville - Birthplace of the Canadian Flag"
Corporate Branding
Moved by: Councillor Beatty

THAT Council endorse the use of the tagline "Brockville - Birthplace of the Canadian
Flag’; and

THAT the design and guidelines for use of the tagline "Brockville - Birthplace of the
Canadian Flag" be developed by staf.

CARRIED
Councilor Earle distributed stickers stating "Brockville - Birthplace of the Canadian
Flag’. He does not see a branding exercise as being necessary to use "Brockville
Birthplace of the Canadian Flag" on Corporation marketing material, letterhead, etc.

Councilor Earle and Councillor McFall noted that they would prefer to fund the flag
festival rather than undertake a branding exercise.

Ms. MacDonald explained that a branding exercise sets the how and when "Brockville
Birthplace of the Canadian Flag" would be used in addition to or in replacement of the
‘existing brands, taglines and logos, and would include the design of a graphic.
