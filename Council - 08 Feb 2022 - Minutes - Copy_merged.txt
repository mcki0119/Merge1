2022-6
Official Plan Amendment and Zoning By-law Amendment
Brockville Shopping Centre - 125 Stewart Boulevard

THAT Official Plan Amendment No. 22 respecting lands described as Part of
Lots 5 and 6, Plan 238, and Part of Lot 12, Concession 1, as in LR133104,
LR74470, LR41159, except LR66143, S/T LR127622, City of Brockville,
County of Leeds, with municipal address of 125 Stewart Boulevard, be
amended, to:

+ Redesignate the property from "Mixed Use and Commercial Area”,
located within the "Mixed Use Node" subject to "Source Water
Protection Considerations" to "Mixed Use and Commercial Area - Site
Specific Policy Area", located within the "Mixed Use Node" subject to
“Source Water Protection Considerations";

+ Add a new site-specific policy to permit residential occupancy of the
ground floor without the requirement to meet a minimum ground
floor height of 4.5 metres in relation to the residential units, only;
and,

‘+ Identify a maximum height of 12 storeys.

THAT Zoning By-law 050-2014, be amended, to rezone lands described as
Part of Lots 5 and 6, Plan 238, and Part of Lot 12, Concession 1, as in
LR133104, LR74470, LR41159, except LR66143, S/T LR127622, City of
Brockville, County of Leeds, with municipal address of 125 Stewart
Boulevard, from "MC - Mixed Use Corridor Zone", subject to "Source Water
Protection” considerations, to "MC - Mixed Use Corridor Special Exception
Zone", subject to "Source Water Protection” considerations, to allow
redevelopment of the subject lands for a mixed-use centre containing a
variety of commercial uses, 2 residential towers (to a maximum of 36.0
metres), and to establish site specific development standards.
