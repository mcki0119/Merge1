MAYOR'S REMARKS.

Mayor Kalivas commented on what a nice weekend it was and how great it was to
see so many people down on the waterfront, taking part in many activities.

His Worship discussed the devastation in the Ottawa area and throughout Ontario,
following the weekend storms. He stated that our thoughts are with all the people
and families affected.

Mayor Kalivas announced that things are really opening up. He listed the events
that are planned throughout the summer. Mayor Kalivas noted that it is the many
volunteers that make all this happen.
