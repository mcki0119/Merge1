MAYOR'S REMARKS

Mayor Wren welcomed everyone to the meeting. A special thanks was expressed
to City staff for the maintenance of the roads during the Christmas 2022 winter
storm. He explained what a significant weather event and asked the residents for
patience during these events. The Brockville Museum was thanked for hosting the
New Year's Day Levy.

His Worship explained the changes to the garbage collection schedule that become
effective February 6, 2023.

+ “That prior to final approval of the Plan, traffic calming measures be

policy, with an emphasis on replacement of trees within the immediate
neighbourhood;

THAT active transportation infrastructure be prioritized in the final design

THAT the necessary By-laws be enacted
