MAYOR'S REMARKS

Mayor Baker congratulated Kollage on the opening of their manufacturers site at
Career Services. They produce square knitting needles. It was noted that it is
pothole season and drivers are asked to be patient during these mild temperatures
until City crews can get the potholes repaired. His Worship spoke about the
increase in use of the Rotary Park ice surface and the need to publish a schedule
for various uses.

DISCLOSURE OF INTEREST
Nil.

Kin Canada

February 16th to 22nd, 2020 Kin Canada Week in the City of Brockville.
