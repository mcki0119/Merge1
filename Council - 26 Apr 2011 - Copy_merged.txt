1. South East LHIN - Councillor Earle
Moved by: Councillor Earle
WHEREAS we understand a Consultant's report to the South East LHIN
recommends the reduction or closure of surgical units at the Brockville General
Hospital and other Eastern Ontario hospitals; and
THAT we the representatives of the citizens of Brockville demand that this and
any other similar recommendations be immediately withdrawn. Our citizens in
‘municipalities have donated millions of dollars and countless hours in the support
‘of our hospitals. We expect them to be full service hospitals for our needs. We do
riot expect basic surgical procedures to involve a road trip to another
municipality; and
FURTHER THAT this resolution be circulated to all municipaities within Leeds
and Grenville, the South East LHIN, the Premier, the Minister of Health and Long
‘Term Care, MPP Steve Clark and hospitals within the South East LHIN.
CARRIED
