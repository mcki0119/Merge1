MAYOR'S REMARKS

‘The Mayor explained that the announced closure of Abbott Labs is as a result of the
reorganization of their entire company. It was announced that 3,000 employees will
be laid off world-wide with approximately 150 employees as a result of the closure in
Brockville. The City is working with Provincial and Federal governments to assist
with the closure including potential new businesses for the plant.

Mayor Henderson announced that the Rotary Clubs will be taking on the
‘organization of the Santa Claus Parade this year. Further information will be made
public shortly

His Worship explained that the proposed new Official Plan is not listed for discussion
‘on the agenda this evening. It is proposed to be discussed at the Council meeting of
October 12th,

‘The Mayor noted that City staff are working with the staff of the United Counties of
Leeds Grenville on waste management plans. In addition, the Counties have
‘announced funding for two playground structures to be located within the City. The
‘County has also formed a Committee regarding the Ash Borer threat
