MAYOR'S REMARKS.

The Mayor updated the public on the survey that has been launched with regards
to the old Woolworths building on King Street, seeking input for the revitalization
of the property. To date there have been over 575 responses received, he
reminded anyone interested in completing the survey can do so on the City's
website until March 12th.

The Mayor offered thanks to all the homeowners and businesses that are helping
with the snow removal in their general area. In addition, all the people who are
keeping their vehicles off the roads especially overnight to, to aid in snow
plowing and removal

Mayor Baker offered his gratitude and appreciation to all the volunteers and
board members of the Aquatarium and our Community Partnerships.

Councillor Earle entered the meeting at 6:10 pm.

DISCLOSURE OF INTEREST

Councillor Bursey declared a potential indirect pecuniary interest on the YMCA’s
presentation as R. Adams, Chief Executive Officer at the YMCA, also sits on a
Board of Directors that oversees his employment.
