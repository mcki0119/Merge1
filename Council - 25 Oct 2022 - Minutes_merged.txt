MAYOR'S REMARKS.

His Worship congratulated Mayor Elect Matt Wren and all the incumbents who
were re-elected. Additionally, he congratulated Louise Severson and Katherine
Hobbs for their successful campaigns and stated he is looking forward to the next

4 years.

Mayor Kalivas shared that at the Chamber of Commerce's Annual Gala former
Mayor Jason Baker was awarded Citizen of the Year, and Northern Cables received
the award for Business of the year.

Mayor Kalivas commented on the banners that the Royal Canadian Legion has
installed on King Street. His Worship stated that each banner represents a veteran
Who served in the Canadian Military. He noted they should make us all proud. His
worship felt it was a great initiative leading up to Remembrance Day.
