MAYOR'S REMARKS.

Mayor Baker reminded the citizens that winter is upon us and winter road/sidewalk
maintenance has begun. His Worship asked that the streets be left clear of cars
overnight, especially in the downtown area, for ease of snow plowing and removal.
Also, schools are starting and finishing at different times this year, he asked that
people who are out on the roads please be mindful of students walking to and
from school.

His Worship announced that this Friday, November 27th, at 6 o'clock pm the
Christmas tree over the fountain at Courthouse will be turned on. Mayor Baker
stated that the Brockville Rotary Club has done a fabulous job modifying the Santa
Claus Parade to make sure a COVID-19 safe version will happen. This will happen
Saturday, November 28", beginning at 5 pm. on Blockhouse Island in conjunction
with the River of Lights kick off.

Mayor Baker noted that the three meals over three days started today and finishes
up on Thursday. He noted seeing several posts, on social media, of people sharing
the meals they were having at various restaurants and eateries throughout the
City.

Mayor Baker announced that on December 1, 2020 staff will be presenting an
update to the staff organizational structural. This new structure’s emphasis Is to
modernize how the City is run and make it more efficient.
