Provision of feminine products at City Facilities
(Councillor Bursey)

Moved by: Councillor Bursey

WHEREAS conversations about providing feminine hygiene products are
currently taking place in municipalities across the province of Ontario; and

WHEREAS we strive to make this Corporation a safe and inclusive place to
live, work and play;

‘THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED THAT the Corporation of the City of Brockville
explore the feasibility of expanding the offer of free menstrual products to all
public-facing municipal buildings, with the direct intention of implementation
as soon as reasonably possible

REFERRED ON A LATER VOTE

Motion to Refer
Moved by: Councillor Kalivas
That the Provision of feminine products at City Facilities be referred to staff

to investigate costs and feasibility of implementing this service.

Ontario Senior Games Bid

Canada Culture Spaces Fund Application

‘THAT City Council approve the submission of an application to Heritage

Feasibility Study for the Brockville Museum building; and

THAT the Director of Corporate Service be authorized to review and execute
the application; and

THAT the Brockville Museum Board be invited to a future Committee meeting
to update City Council on their future plans and the status of the grant
application prior to entering into a Funding Agreement.
