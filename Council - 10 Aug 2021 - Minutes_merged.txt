SR2021-142
Zoning By-law Amendment - 20 Ann Street

THAT Council approve the application to rezone the lands with municipal
address 20 Ann Street from "R3-General Residential Zone", subject to
"Source Water Protection Considerations" to "R3-General Residential
Zone", in part, and "R1-Single Detached Residential Zone", in part, subject
to "Source Water Protection Considerations" to facilitate a proposed
consent of the rear lands and to convey them to 1 Granite Street East; and

THAT the necessary by-laws be enacted.

SR2021-146
Cost Overrun Guarantee request
Marguerita Residence Corporation
42 Garden St Project

THAT Council receives report $R2021-146 Marguerita Residence
Corporation 42 Garden St. Cost Overrun Guarantee request; and

THAT Council approves a Cost Overrun Guarantee with the Marguerita
Residence Corporation of up to $750,000; and

THAT Council direct Staff to formalize the terms for the Cost Overrun
Guarantee; and

THAT Council authorize the Mayor and Clerk to execute the agreement.

SR2021-147
2021 Transfer Payment Agreement re Clock Tower Revitalization Funding

THAT Council receives report $R2021-147 2021 Transfer Payment
Agreement re Clock Tower Revitalization; and

THAT Council authorizes the Mayor and Clerk to enter into a Transfer
Payment Agreement with Her Majesty the Queen in right of Ontario as
represented by the Minister of Infrastructure for the Investing in Canada
Infrastructure Program (ICIP); COVID 19 Resilience Infrastructure Stream
for $105,532; and

THAT the necessary by-law be enacted.
