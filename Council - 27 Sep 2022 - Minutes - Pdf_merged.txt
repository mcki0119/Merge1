MAYOR'S REMARKS.

Mayor Kalivas remarked the devastation that hurricane Fiona has left behind in the
Atlantic region. He was glad to see our Armed Forces are moving in to help.

Councillor Journal reminded everyone that the Harvest Sharing Food Drive is
coming up. It runs from October 3rd to the 8th and supports Brockville and Area
and South Grenville food banks. Councillor Journal described the many ways to
donate food and make monetary donations. He challenged Council and Staff to
match his twenty-dollar donation.
