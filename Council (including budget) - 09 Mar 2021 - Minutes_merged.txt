MAYOR'S REMARKS.

Mayor Baker expressed condolences to the Fraser family. Brian Fraser lost his
battle with Leukemia last week. Brian was an advocate for blood and stem cell
donations.

Brockville flags will be flown at half mast on Thursday March 11th, 2021 to
recognize the designation by the federal government as the National Day of
Observance to commemorate the people who lost their lives and the significant
Impacts we have all felt because of COVID-19.
