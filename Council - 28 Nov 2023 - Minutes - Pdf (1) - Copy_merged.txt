Mayor Wren shared at their own expense, Leeds Pharmacy has contracted
with the “Good Doctors” group to offer a by appointment virtual clinic at
their location in the Ormond Street Plaza. He explained the program
supports the establishment of an on-site nurse practitioner three day per
week. He added that this will certainly help fill a gap for many people who
would otherwise be having to use the emergency department.

His Worship explained that city staff have arranged to keep the North
‘Augusta Road compost site open for an additional two weeks and said yard
waste and leaves may be dropped off to the site at the Gord Watts Centre
until December 8th. Details can be found on the City website news section
‘The mayor reminded the public of the Centeen Park Public Information
Session regarding the proposed new Sewage Pumping Station being held
this Thursday, November 30th from 4-7 pm in Council Chambers.

Mayor Wren remarked that they will be appointing a new City Clerk,
Sheena Earl, in this evening's agenda. Ms. Earl is joining the city next
week after working as Clerk of the United Counties of Leeds and Grenville.
