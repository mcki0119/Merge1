1. SR2021-106
Risk Management Official and Inspector Services

THAT Report SR2021-106, Risk Management Official and Inspector
Services be received; and

THAT Council approves the transfer of responsibilities, as it relates to Part
IV of the Clean Water Act, 2006, for the enforcement of regulating drinking
water threats within the City of Brockville to Cambium Inc. for a three (3)
year period ending December 31, 2023.

SR2021-100
Zoning By-law Amendment - 108 Perth Street

THAT Zoning By-law 050-2014, be amended, to rezone lands with
municipal address 108 Perth Street from "C1-7-Local Commercial Special
Exception Zone", subject to "Source Water Protection Considerations" to
“I1-General Institutional Special Exception Zone" subject to "Source Water
Protection Considerations" to permit a "Place of Worship" on the property
and to recognize existing setbacks, parking lot location, and existing
planting strip and yard requirements.

SR2021-103
Request to Amend Parking By-Law 119-89

THAT Council receive Report SR2021-103; and

THAT Council authorize the installation of six (6) ‘No Parking’ signs on
Edward Street, near the intersection with Jessie Street; and

THAT Schedule “5” Subsection 74 of By-Law 119-89, regulating Parking
within the City of Brockville be amended accordingly.
