‘Councilor Fullarton asked for an update on the Fiscal Policy Reserve balance. Mr.
Casselman explained that there is a recommendation to transfer $500,000 from the
Hydro One reserve to the Fiscal Policy Reserve. If the recommendation was approved
the fund would have a balance of approximately $600,000.

Councilor Baker recalled that the Hydro One funds were earmarked 0 ald in offsetting
increases to particular tax classes. Mr. Casselman clarified that there was a contribution
from the Hydro One proceeds allotted to the water pollution control centre upgrade
project.

Councilor Journal recollected that the Hydro One residual balance was tagged to pay
the interest charges related to the pollution project which would be of greater benefit to
the residential property owners.

‘The Clerk noted to Members of Council that at the Council meeting of June 24, 2008,
Council passed a recommendation that allocated the balance of the Hydro One Funds
{o future capital projects.

In ight of the information provided by the Clerk Councillor Journal moved a motion for
reconsideration,

‘Suspend the rules of procedure to consider a motion for reconsideration

Reconsideration Respecting
Use of Hydro One funds

Moved by: Councillor Journal
THAT Council suspend the rules of procedure as set out in the By-law Governing the
Proceedings of Council, as amended, to reconsider a motion related to the use of the
remaining Hydro One funds, passed at the Council meeting of June 24, 2008.

WITHDRAWN
Members discussed the appropriateness of using the funds in the manner as set forth in
the motion. Councilor and Fullarton noted that the balance of the Hydro One funds
were intended to fund a community capital project, such as the splash pad and not to
fund the Fiscal Policy Reserve nor the Maritime Discovery Centre.

Alter discussions, Councillor Journal withdrew his motion.
