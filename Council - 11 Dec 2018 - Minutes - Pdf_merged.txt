Private Cannabis Retail Stores "Opt-in" or "Opt-out"

comment on the matter of whether the City of Brockville should "Opt-
Out" of permitting Private Cannabis Retail Stores within the limits of the
Corporation of the City of Brockville;

2. THAT the Corporation of the City of Brockville not “Opt-Out” of permitting
Private Cannabis Retail Stores within the limits of the Corporation of the
City of Brockville;

3. THAT Staff develop a Municipal Cannabis Policy Statement to be brought
forward to Council at a future date; and

4. THAT J. Faurschou, Planner I, and A. McGinnis, Planner II, be named as
contacts to provide a one-window approach to co-ordinate municipal input
to the Alcohol and Gaming Commission of Ontario (AGCO) regarding
proposed cannabis stores.

2. 2018-128-12
Proposed Allocation of the Municipal Accommodation Tax

Moved by: Councillor Fullarton

THAT the following allocation of the Municipal Accommodation Tax (MAT) be
approved;

Funds received from the MAT, as at December 31st each year, will be
allocated as follows:

1. 50% to the local Destination Marketing Organization (DMO) for
increased marketing, support of local festivals and new product
development.

2. 50% to the City of Brockville for tourism-related assets and activities.

THAT $50,000 of the 2018 MAT receipts, before the formula is applied, is

allocated to the Aquatarium as authorized by Council resolution at the
Council meeting of Tuesday, July 24th, 2018. Of the balance of the 2018

MAT receipts, the City of Brockville’s 50% shall be allocated to the
Aquatarium;

THAT the DMO report to EDRTC annually with a costed business plan of how
the MAT funds will be spent;

THAT the DMO report to EDRTC annually with the outcomes from the money
spent;

THAT the DMO will provide annual audited financial statements to the City of
Brockville related to MAT revenues and expenses;

THAT no fee be charged by the City for administration of the MAT.
