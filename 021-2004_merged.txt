‘THE CORPORATION OF THE CITY OF BROCKVILLE
By-Law Number 021-2004
By-law to Amend By-law Number 128-2003, being a by-law

to Appoint Members to Various Boards and
Commissions of the City of Brockville

WHEREAS By-law Number 128-2003 appoints membership to certain Boards and
‘Commissions; and

WHEREAS due to openings on certain Boards and Commissions, it is necessary
to amend By-law 128-2003;

NOW THEREFORE THE COUNCIL OF THE CORPORATION OF THE CITY OF
BROCKVILLE ENACTS AS FOLLOWS.

1. THAT Mariko Boag, Cliff Knox, Trish Galvin and Dave Cody be added to Schedule
D of By-law 128-2003(Performing and Visual Arts Advisory Committee) with their
terms to expire November 30, 2008; and

2 THAT Cliff Knox be added to Schedule J (Property Standards Committee) with his
term to expire November 30, 2008; and

3. THAT Jennifer Cullen be added to Schedule H (Brockville Public Library Board)
with her term to expire November 30, 2008: and

4. THAT Mr. John Bonser be added to Schedule M (Cataraqui Region Conservation
Authority) for a term to expire on November 30, 2004,

Given under the Seal of the
Corporation of the City of Brockville
and passed this 9 Day of March, 2004

‘Mayor’
