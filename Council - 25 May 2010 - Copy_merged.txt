2010-082-06
Non-Resident User Fee

Moved by: Councillor Beach

THAT Council authorizes staff to begin comprehensive engagement with all
stakeholder groups regarding the Non-Resident Use Fee (NRUF) in order to
bring forward a solution to the problems associated with the NRUF for
Council's consideration. This would include all sports leagues operating
within the City's recreational facilites, and the outside municipalities where
non-resident users reside; and

‘THAT until such time as a new arrangement can be reached; the status quo
is recommended for this policy as opposed to abolishment.

CARRIED

residents to vote with assistance from Staff
